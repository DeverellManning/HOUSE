
)who:action "p1" p2~"Message";

Where	'who' is the character it came from,
	'action' is what they did to you,
	'p1' is a parameter in quotes,
	'p2' is also a parameter,
	'Message' is the message to be displayed by the recipient.

Examples of Transfer Format:
)deverell:hug~"Deverell hugs you tightly!";
